MythicMobs Integration

When MythicMobs is installed, DirectorsCut registers a mechanic named DirectorsCut (also accepted as Action) that can trigger sequences for a specific player.

Example MythicMobs skill

Skills:
  - DirectorsCut{sequence=castle_intro;player=<target.name>}

Targeter context

During this skill, the entity targeted by MythicMobs becomes the mythicmobs_targeter. Cameras can anchor or look at it with source: mythicmobs_targeter.

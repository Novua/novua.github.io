Anchor/Look Relativity

Camera relativity is controlled by anchor and look_at target definitions.

  • Player-relative: source: self.
  • Mob-relative: source: <spawn alias> from spawnmob.
  • Absolute-world: use type: absolute with explicit coordinates.
anchor:
  type: entity
  source: boss
look_at:
  type: entity
  source: boss
positioning:
  mode: relative
  distance: 4
  y_offset: 2
  yaw_offset: 180

To retarget a camera from player to mob, usually only change source; keep positioning values.

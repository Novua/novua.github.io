End Conditions

Add an endconditions list to define alternate ways to end a sequence early.

endconditions:
  - sneak
  - offhandswap
  - hotbarkey(2)

Supported end conditions

  • sneak — Stop when the player sneaks.
  • offhandswap — Stop when the player swaps offhand items.
  • hotbarkey(slot) — Stop when a specific hotbar slot is selected (0-8).

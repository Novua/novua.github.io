Troubleshooting checklist

Troubleshooting & debugging

Track down issues by enabling debug logging and checking the relevant YAML entries.

  • MythicMob not found: Enable debug-mode to see spawn attempts and verify the MythicMob ID in YAML matches your Mythic configuration.
  • Animations fail in certain worlds: Make sure the world name is not listed under disabled_worlds.
  • Permissions not recognised: Confirm the player has the nodes defined under an animation's permissions array or grant animcore.admin.bypass while testing.
  • AFK never fires: Ensure AFKTimeout is reasonable and no plugins spam activity events (mount movement, anti-AFK scripts, etc.).
  • Spawn teleport fails: Re-run /animcore setspawn to refresh coordinates and confirm the destination world is loaded.
